# STILL UNDER HEAVILY DEVELOPMENT

## FAQ?
### What is the goal of PPC?
The goal is to fill the region of Chernarus with AI from HunterZ AI Bandits mod, to test a push the boundaries of this mod and see how much of a "living world" we can make the world feel without having a strain on a dedicated server like the Expansion AI Mod.

### So what's the plan exactly?
Every square inch of Chernarus will have a story or patrol what makes this unique is the scale this can bring to the world as the mod is so lightweight compared to other AI mods it's worth giving a try to see if it is possible.

Split the map into the corresponding loot tiers and do one at a time starting with tier 1

Write a wiki page explaining the "lore" of the world and what to expect at each area.

### How far have you got?
I have toyed with a couple of different scenarios such as a heavily guarded boss location, nature trail walkers and POI groups to see how there navigation is, but until I complete a town or two it is very early days.

I am also testing some theories and trialing some additional mods which brings expansion to AI Bandits such as "Knock Knock AI" & "Bandits Unlesshed" but so far under testing it works but its needs some tweaking.
